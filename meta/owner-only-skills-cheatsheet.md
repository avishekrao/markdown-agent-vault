# Memo owner-only skills

## Essence

A quick reminder: for development tasks, the agent should take care of reading, editing, reviewing and reporting, and the owner makes decisions only at risk points.

## Details

- `owner-only-dev-orchestrator` - manages the entire development task.
- `test-gates` - checks the quality of changes.
- `release-rollback` - helps make the decision to release or rollback.

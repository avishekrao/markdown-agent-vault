# Context

## The essence
Stable project invariants in one place: terms, metrics, SoT, constraints. **Does not store an episode from meetings** - it lives in the files of the meetings themselves under the `## Mentioned in passing` canonical tag. A transfer trigger from an episodic to `context.md` is the moment the owner/agent formulates an opinion ("we know X is Y") rather than a mention counter.

See [task-routing-methodology-2026-04.md §8](../../03_knowledge/task-routing-methodology-2026-04.md). Goal / Non-goals / Milestones do not live here; they live in [plan.md](./plan.md).

# GAP Interview Questions

> Navigation: [SKILL](../SKILL.md)]

Use when the data is not enough for a reproducible case.

## Context Gaps
- What was the initial goal and criterion of success?
- What restrictions were critical (time, budget, access)?

## Method Gaps
- What exact steps were taken in order?
- Which Settings/Settings gave the best result?
- What did you try and give up?

## Metrics Gaps
- What are the before/after metrics?
- How was the result measured and at what time?

## Transferability Gaps
- For which teams / scripts is this case not suitable?
- What needs to be adapted to a different context?

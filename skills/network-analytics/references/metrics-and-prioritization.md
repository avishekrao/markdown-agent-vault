# Metrics and Prioritization

> Navigation: [SKILL](../SKILL.md)]

## Metric Interpretation

### Degree centrality
It shows who has more direct connections.

### Betweenness centrality
It shows who is the bridge between the clusters.

### Eigenvector centrality
Shows influence through connections with already influential nodes.

### Communities
Show domains / hustle, where the connections are denser than average.

## Outreach Prioritization

Prioritize this:
1. High betweenness + Relevant Domain
2. Medium betweenness and High Trust Path (Warm Intro)
3. High degree within the target cluster

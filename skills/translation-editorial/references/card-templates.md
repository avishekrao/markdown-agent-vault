## Short Digest Card

```markdown
### <A short title essentially >>

**What happened: ** <1-2 sentences >>

**Why is this important in the work: * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * >>

** Who benefits:** <segments >>

**What to do in 7 days: ** <1 concrete step >>

**What not to do:** <typical error >>
```

## Blog Paragraph

```markdown
Fact and context in 2-3 sentences.

In practice, this changes the process/role: before it was, now it is. This is especially useful for the audience because it has a specific effect.

Minimum pilot per week: "Steps". Don't "anti-pattern."
```

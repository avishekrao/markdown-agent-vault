# Editorial Checklist

> Navigation: [SKILL](../SKILL.md)]

## Clarity
- The subject is clear from the first paragraph.
- There are no unexplained terms.
- One thought per paragraph.

## Practicality
- There is an effect on the process/role.
- There is action in the horizon of 7 days.
- There are limitations/risks of misuse.

## Language
- There is no office or marketing cliches.
- There are no best/revolutionary statements without provable mechanics.
- There are no passive language that hides responsibility.
